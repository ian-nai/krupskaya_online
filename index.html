Nadezhda Krupskaya: An Online Resource

This site is intended to provide an online home for English-language resources relating to Nadezhda Krupskaya, Deputy Education Commissar of the Soviet Union from 1929 to 1939, who played a vital role in the development and support of Soviet librarianship and libraries.

If you would like to make a suggestion for additional resources to add, please feel free to send me a message.
